Privacy Policy

At Age Calculator, we value the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Age Calculator and how we use it.

Data Collection

We do not store any user data. The date of birth you enter into our calculator is processed entirely on the client side (in your browser) and is not saved anywhere on our servers.

Cookies and Advertisements

Cookies may be used by third-party ads. Google AdSense, as a third-party vendor, may use cookies to serve personalized ads to our users. You may choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options.

Information Safety

Your data is safe and not shared with any third parties. Since we do not collect any personal information via the calculator, there is no risk of data leakage from our side.

Log Files

Age Calculator follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Consent
